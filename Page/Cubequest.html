üéÆ Presentation

Cubequest is a puzzle-platformer where you play as Cube, a robot from the future. The goal is to traverse three distinct worlds to collect essential parts and return to the starting point.

The gameplay is built on an original concept: the player possesses a numerical "value" (between 1 and 9), which affects their size, interactions, and abilities.

  • Dynamic Score: The cube displays a value between 1 and 9. If this value falls outside this range, the player dies.
  • Size Changing:

    • 7‚Äì9: Large
  • Colored Batteries: These modify the player's value using operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division), with each color representing a different effect.
  • Interactive Ball: The player can throw a ball whose range depends on their size. This ball can also interact with batteries and solve puzzles from a distance.
  • Systemic Interaction: Most game mechanics can interact with one another, leading to dynamic and creative puzzles.
