🦇 Bat Cave Defender

Bat Cave Defender is a simple 2D arcade-style game where you control a bat defending its cave from waves of incoming insects. The bat remains stationary inside the cave and attacks enemies approaching from three sides — left, right, and above. Enemies cannot come from below.

🎮 Gameplay

  • The bat protects the cave.
  • Insects attack from three directions: left, right, and top.
  • Press directional keys to attack in the direction of the incoming insect.
  • Occasionally, a heart will float toward the bat. Don’t attack it — letting it reach you will restore some health.
  • The longer you survive, the faster and harder the game becomes!

🛠️ Features

  • Fast-paced 2D arcade action
  • Directional attack system (left, right, and up)
  • Strategic healing mechanic (non-violent interaction)
  • Increasing difficulty for higher replayability

🚀 How to Play

  • Start the game.
  • Use the arrow keys (← ↑ →) to attack in the direction of approaching enemies.
  • Let hearts reach you to heal — don’t attack them!
  • Survive as long as possible while protecting your cave.
